Technical info

WAV metadata chunks

    Convert

    The FFmpeg engine (~31 MB) is downloaded once when you first convert, then cached by your browser.

    Operations

      Privacy: all processing happens locally in your browser. Audio and images are never uploaded anywhere. The only network request after page load is the one-time FFmpeg engine download (on first conversion).

      MP3 → WAV decodes to PCM without further quality loss (it cannot restore what MP3 already discarded). WAV → MP3 is lossy re-encoding.
